Mother Earth

About Mother Earth

Mother Earth, also known as Earth Mother, is the third planet from the sun in the Solar System that we live in. As far as we know, it is the only planet to be found that supports life. Mother Earth provides us with water and air, which are very essential for life, without it, no living being would survive without it. Mother Earth is also a Greek goddess of Earth, Gaia. Gaia was the Greek goddess of Earth, mother of all life, similar to the Roman Terra Mater (Mother Earth).

How can we save Mother Earth?

The 3Rs - Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Reduce Reuse Recycle
Reduce means to reduce the amount of waste we throw away. Reuse means to find new ways to use things that we trow away. Recycle means to turn something useless into something useful.
Examples: Don't throw away plastic, instead use eco-friendly items like cloth, rubber or paper Examples: Use things that can be reused such as handkerchiefs, natural rubber bottles, etc Examples: Instead of throwing stuff away, use your creativity and turn them into something useful

Conserving Mother Earth

Earth's natural resources include air, water, soil, minerals, plants, and animals. Conservation is the practice of caring for these resources so all living things can benefit from them now and in the future.

How can we protect our Earth?

We can do simple things to protect our Earth such as planting a tree or any plant, conserving water and using it only when we need such as taking shorter showers and using a bucket and a mug to bath, shopping wisely and buying less plastic, not throwing waste or any other harmful materials into the water bodies such as plastic, glass, or any other harmful chemical, and many more.
